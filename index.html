Poppy Seed Chicken

Who doesn't love good homestyle cooking? Certainly not me. With this delicious recipe you will be able to create a simple but tasty dish that is just as good as moms home cooking. Simple and easy to make any night of the week and there will be leftovers to spare.

•Ingredients•

  • 3 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 8 oz container of Hood Sour Cream
  • 1 10½ oz can of Campbells Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 2 tsp of poppy seeds
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 cups of crushed Ritz Crackers
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

•Tools You'll Need•

  • Large pot
  • 9x9 inch baking dish

    • •Directions•

    Step One →

    Preheat the oven to 350℉.

    In a large pot, bring water to boil.

    Step Two →

    Place the chicken in the pot and parboil for 20 minutes or until the tempreature reads 165℉.

    Step Three →

    After cooking chicken, drain and let cool. Cut into 1 inch chunks.

    Step Four →

    In a 9x9 inch baking dish combine the cream of chicken soup, the sour cream, poppy seeds and chicken.

    Step Five →

    Melt the butter.

    Combine the melted butter with the crushed Ritz and use the mixture to top the chicken mixture.

    Step Six →

    Bake dish for 45 minutes covered. Then uncover and continue to cook for an additonal 15 minutes. Serve and Enjoy!
